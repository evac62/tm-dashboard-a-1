About

How We Were Born

  • 2003

    Our Humble Beginnings

    The original TWO MAIDS & A MOP office location was located in Pensacola, FL. The tiny 250 square foot office space was located on the wrong side of the tracks – literally. A railroad track stretched across all sides of the property and loud roars could be heard every 30 minutes. This tiny office served as a breeding ground for some of the brand’s most successful initiatives today; including the cleaning industry’s most famous compensation plan: The Pay for Performance Plan.

  • 2004

    Early Challenges

    TWO MAIDS & A MOP opened its second location in Destin, FL. One week after the grand opening, Hurricane Ivan brought category 5 winds to the local area and destroyed hundreds of homes in the area; including many of our client’s homes. It was a devastating and major blow for the company’s second location. Fortunately, the location is alive and well today and represents a thriving small market territory for TWO MAIDS & A MOP.

  • 2005

    A Scalable Brand Is Born

    Our third location opened along the Gulf Coast of Northwest Florida in beautiful Panama City Beach, FL. It was such a fun time. Three locations; all located within a few miles of the beach. This location proved one important thing: TWO MAIDS & A MOP could be successful in multiple markets and there was no need for the founder to be active in the daily operations of every office. A systemized, scalable brand was being born!

  • 2009

    The Home Office Catalyst

    TWO MAIDS & A MOP opens its first location outside the state of Florida in Birmingham, AL. The city would soon become the company’s home office. Better yet, the Birmingham location would serve as a catalyst for future company growth. The company’s innovative lead generation formula was tested here and results proved to be better than ever expected. The ball was rolling!

  • 2010

    Phase One Expansion

    The fifth and sixth locations open in Nashville, TN and Huntsville, AL. Nashville represented our largest metropolitan market and proved that our concept could work in small, medium and large markets. Lots of home grown talent started to fill our management pipeline during this time so we decided to leverage the talent pipeline and open even more locations beyond our familiar state borders.

  • 2011

    Phase Two Expansion

    Two additional office locations open; Knoxville, TN and Atlanta, GA. Our reach stretched across four states and we eclipsed the 100 employee mark during this year. The growth in each of our locations was overwhelming at this point. We were growing so quickly – everyday! Our growth was finally being recognized by the industry and we started receiving calls on a regular basis from people interested in starting their own cleaning business. We weren’t ready for franchising just yet but the phone calls from interested people planted a seed in our head. What if we let our secret out by franchising the brand across the country?

  • 2012

    Phase Three Expansion

    Three more office locations open; Montgomery, AL; Greensboro, NC and Charlotte, NC. We were cleaning hundreds of homes everyday across five different states. What a tremendous feeling; considering our humble origins. Best of all, an industry association formally recognized TWO MAIDS & A MOP as the National Maid Service of the Year!

  • 2013

    Inc. Magazine - Fastest Growing Cleaning Company

    Chattanooga, TN opens and our first franchised unit also opens in Tampa, FL. We received a number of awards during this year but the biggest one came from Inc. Magazine. The magazine recognized our company as the fastest growing cleaning company in the nation.

  • 2014

    National Publicity

    Two additional franchise locations open for business; Fort Worth, TX and Washington, D.C. In addition, two existing affiliate locations in Tennessee were converted into franchise locations. TWO MAIDS & A MOP garners national publicity with feature articles in CNN Money, Yahoo Finance, and Entrepreneur Magazine.

  • 2015

    Speeding Into The Future

    Rapid expansion of the brand occurs with multiple locations opening in Raleigh, NC; Dallas, TX; Tallahassee, FL; Jacksonville, FL; Tuscaloosa, AL; Suwanee, GA; and Alpharetta, GA.Several affiliate locations are converted into franchise units including Knoxville, TN; Montgomery, AL; Destin, FL; and the original location in Pensacola, FL. National publicity continues with more feature articles in Success Magazine, Inc. Magazine, and Better Homes and Gardens. The brand is prominently featured on the Nasdaq Tower in Times Square! Want to learn more about the TWO MAIDS & A MOP™ franchise opportunity? Visit the franchise opportunity website for more details.
